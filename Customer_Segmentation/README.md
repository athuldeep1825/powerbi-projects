Project Name: Customer Segmentation & Shopping Trends Analysis

Tools Used:

* Power BI
* Excel
* Power Query
* DAX

Description:

This project analyzes U.S. retail transaction data to understand customer purchasing behavior, promotional effectiveness, and seasonal demand patterns.
The study focuses on aggregating customer-level metrics and segmenting customers into meaningful groups to support data-driven marketing strategies and revenue optimization.

Key Insights:

* Identified high-revenue product categories and discount-responsive segments
* Analyzed revenue contribution across seasons, locations, and age groups
* Segmented customers into behavior-based groups such as Discount Driven, Promo Driven, Price Sensitive, and Premium customers
* Evaluated dominant promotional factors influencing customer purchase decisions
* Designed targeted marketing strategies tailored to each customer segment

Outcome:
Enabled actionable insights for targeted marketing campaigns
optimized promotional planning
improved understanding of customer value and behavior using interactive Power BI dashboards.
